Why Sip Sober exists

Because “I’m not drinking tonight” shouldn’t mean you’re stuck with water. Sip Sober helps you find places that take zero-proof seriously — with cocktails built to match the moment, not just the menu.

For sober, sober-curious, and “just not tonight”

We map spots that respect your choice.

We focus on venues that offer thoughtful zero-proof options: balanced spritzes, spirit-free riffs on classics, and drinks built with brands like Seedlip, Ritual Zero Proof, and Lyre’s.

No judgments, no labels. Just better nights out — minus the hangover.

How the app works

Sip Sober is designed to feel like a calm, focused map — no clutter, no noise. Just filters that make sense and a list you can actually use on your way out the door.

1
Choose a neighborhood.

Start in the Upper East Side, Midtown East, or Gramercy. The map locks to Manhattan so you don’t end up zoomed out over the whole planet.

2
Filter by brand & vibe.

Want a Lyre’s spritz? Ritual for something more spirit-forward? Pick your brands and see where they’re pouring — all in one view.

3
Tap, save, and go.

Tap a spot to see details, save it to your favorites, and use the website like a lightweight app from your home screen.

App preview
Built to feel like a map-first app, not a website.

The full Sip Sober experience opens in a clean, app-style view — perfect for using on the way to dinner, at the bar, or on the couch planning your next night out.

Mobile-first design
Works from your home screen

Add Sip Sober to your phone as a shortcut and it behaves like an app — no downloads, no accounts required just to peek at the map.
