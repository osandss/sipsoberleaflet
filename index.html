What is Sip Sober?

A map-based guide for anyone who wants to drink less — or not at all — without giving up going out. Exceptional zero-proof drinks, real NYC energy — without the pressure.

We highlight NYC bars and restaurants offering balanced spritzes, spirit-free classics, and thoughtful zero-proof cocktails made with real craft spirits.

Whether you’re sober, sober-curious, or skipping alcohol for the night, Sip Sober helps you plan outings that still feel fun.

Features

Built to be simple enough to use while you’re walking down the street — but detailed enough to actually help you choose where to go.

Feature
Browse by neighborhood

Start in the Upper East Side, Midtown East, or Gramercy. The map stays locked on Manhattan so you’re never zooming in from outer space.

Feature
See which brands are pouring

Look for venues pouring Seedlip, Ritual Zero Proof, Lyre’s, and more — so you know what kind of zero-proof experience to expect before you arrive.

Feature
Save your go-to spots

Create an account to favorite venues and build your own list of places you trust for zero-proof drinks, from date-night spots to casual neighborhood hangs.

How it works

Sip Sober lives in your browser but behaves like a lightweight app you can keep on your home screen.

Open the map, choose your neighborhood, and toggle any brands you’re excited about. You’ll see a focused list of venues with zero-proof options and a simple way to tap through to their site or directions.

Want to keep track of your favorites?
You’ll always be able to open the map without an account. But if you want to save spots and build your own zero-proof list, you’ll be able to create a profile soon.
