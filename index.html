What is Sip Sober?

NYC’s zero-proof discovery app for finding bars and restaurants with real non-alcoholic options.

Menus rarely show non-alcoholic options clearly, which makes choosing where to go surprisingly hard. Sip Sober solves that by showing what’s actually available—before you get there.
