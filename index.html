About Us

Welcome to your ultimate guide to navigating the world of zero-proof spirits. Our mission is to help those who choose not to drink uncover bars and restaurants that offer top-tier alcohol-free options.

We highlight venues that take their zero-proof menus seriously — from Lyre’s spritzes to Seedlip-based cocktails and Ritual Zero Proof classics. Whether you’re sober, sober-curious, or “just not tonight,” Sip Sober makes planning a night out feel easy again.

THE MOVEMENT

Why zero-proof matters

For years, going out in NYC meant going out for a drink. As the sober-curious movement grows, more people are choosing to drink less — or not at all — without giving up the city’s energy, restaurants, and late-night plans.

Sip Sober is here to make that choice feel normal, not complicated. You don’t need a reason or a label — just a place that knows how to make a great drink without alcohol.
