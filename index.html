What is Sip Sober?

A map-based guide for anyone who wants to drink less — or not at all — without giving up going out. No labels, no lectures. Just places that take their zero-proof menu seriously.

We curate NYC bars and restaurants that offer thoughtful non-alcoholic drinks: balanced spritzes, spirit-free riffs on classics, and cocktails built with brands like Seedlip, Ritual Zero Proof, and Lyre’s.

Whether you’re sober, sober-curious, or “just not tonight,” Sip Sober makes it easier to plan dates, birthdays, and after-work drinks that still feel like a night out.

How it works

Sip Sober lives in your browser but behaves like a lightweight app you can keep on your home screen.

Open the map, choose your neighborhood, and toggle any brands you’re excited about. You’ll see a focused list of venues with zero-proof options and a simple way to tap through to their site or directions.

Want to keep track of your favorites?
You’ll always be able to open the map without an account. But if you want to save spots and build your own zero-proof list, you’ll be able to create a profile soon.
