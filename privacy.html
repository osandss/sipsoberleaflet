Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Sip Sober respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Sip Sober mobile application.

Information We Collect

Sip Sober may collect the following information:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • User ID
  • Favorites and saved venues

Sip Sober does not collect payment information, contacts, advertising identifiers, or personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Location Information

Sip Sober may request access to your device’s location only if you choose to use the Near Me feature. This allows the app to display venues near your current location.

Location information is used solely to provide this feature and is processed locally by your device. Sip Sober does not store, track, or retain your location data.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Save and display favorite venues
  • Provide core app functionality
  • Allow users to maintain a personalized list of saved locations

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising, marketing, or data brokerage purposes.

Data Storage

User data is securely stored using third-party services that comply with industry-standard security practices.

Third-Party Services

Sip Sober uses third-party services such as Firebase for authentication and data storage. These services may process limited user information as necessary to provide their functionality.

Your Choices

You may remove saved favorites at any time within the app.

Users may also delete their account directly within the application. Deleting an account permanently removes associated account data from Sip Sober's systems.

If you have questions about your data, you may contact us.

Children’s Privacy

Sip Sober is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
